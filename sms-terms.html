Privacy Policy

These SMS Terms & Conditions cover the text messages sent by One Way Insurance. When you opt in to our text program, you're agreeing to what's described here — so please take a moment to read it before giving your consent.

1

Your SMS Consent

The phone number you give us as part of opting in is never shared with third parties for marketing purposes. Your consent is yours alone, and your privacy stays protected.

2

Types of Messages You May Receive

Once you've opted in, the texts from One Way Insurance generally fall into three categories:

Appointment Reminders Example

"Hi [Name], a quick reminder from One Way Insurance about your appointment today at 3:00 PM. Need to reschedule? Just reply or call (646) 384-5570. Reply STOP to opt out of texts anytime."

Follow-Up Messages Example

"Hello [Name], this is [Agent] with One Way Insurance, following up to make sure you received the coverage details we discussed. Questions? Reply here or call (646) 384-5570. Reply STOP to opt out anytime."

Billing Notifications Example

"Hi [Name], a courtesy note from One Way Insurance regarding your recent billing or payment question. For help, call us at (646) 384-5570. Reply STOP to opt out anytime."

3

How Often We'll Text You

The number of texts you receive depends on the type of communication, but we keep it minimal.

A maximum of 2 SMS messages per week, related to your account, appointment reminders, follow-ups, or billing. Message frequency may still vary.

4

Message & Data Rates

Standard message and data rates may apply, based on your carrier's plan. Rates can differ for domestic and international messages, so check with your mobile carrier for specifics.

5

How to Opt In

There are three easy ways to join our SMS program:

By Phone

Tell one of our licensed agents during a call.

Online Form

Check the SMS consent box on a form on our website.

Paper Form

Complete and sign a physical consent form.

6

How to Opt Out

You're always in control. Stop messages whenever you like:

1

Reply STOP

Text STOP to any message and you'll be removed from our list right away.

2

Contact us directly

Prefer to talk to us? Reach out and we'll take you off the list:

(646) 384-5570 contact@oneway-insurance.com
7

Help & Support

If anything about our texts is unclear, you have two quick options:

Reply HELP

Text HELP to any message from us for immediate assistance information.

Visit Our Site

Reach our team anytime through our contact page.

Don't want texts at all? Simply leave the SMS consent box unchecked on our forms — opting in is always optional.

8

Standard Disclosures

Message and data rates may apply. Opt out anytime by texting STOP. For help, text HELP or see our Privacy Policy. Message frequency may vary.
